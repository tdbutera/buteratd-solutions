## F. DITY/PPM — 2026 reimbursement `[Verified – DFAS / DoD MAP 42-25(R), eff 1 Oct 2025]`
- Incentive = **100% of Government Constructed Cost (GCC)** for CY2026 (the temporary **130%** bump, 15 May–30 Sep 2025, **expired** — re-verify at move time).
- **Profit = GCC payment − actual move costs.** GCC scales with **net weight hauled** (capped at allowance) × **distance**.
- **E-8 w/ dependents HHG allowance = 14,000 lb.** `[Verified – JTR 2026]`
- Reimbursement portion is **taxable** — DFAS withholds **22% federal** (no FICA). Offset with documented expenses + certified weigh tickets.
- ⚠️ **~$40K is gross taxable GCC, not take-home** — achievable only near the full 14,000 lb cap over a long move; net is materially lower. Pull personalized GCC from MilMove for the actual lane → `12_Action_Tracker`.

## G. Ch.33 GI Bill — Transfer of Education Benefits (TEB) `[Verified – VA.gov / DoDI 1341.13 / CRS R48178]`
- **HARD RULE: must be submitted AND approved WHILE ON ACTIVE DUTY.** Cannot transfer after retirement (29 Feb 2028) — **non-recoverable deadline.**
- **Designate EVERY dependent pre-separation** (even a $0/1-month placeholder) — you can re-allocate months among already-designated dependents post-retirement, but **cannot add new dependents** later. All dependents must be in **DEERS**.
- Service-obligation: standard 4 additional years from election; **10-year/precluded exemption** likely applies (long service + HYT) — confirm retention status via career counselor.
- Spouse can use immediately; children's use generally after member separates. Layers with Ch.35 DEA (100% P&T) but 48-month combined cap if same person uses both. → time-critical in `12_Action_Tracker`.
