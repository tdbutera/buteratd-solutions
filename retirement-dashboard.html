The whole picture in one breath

  • Two ratings, one fork. 70% vs 100% P&T swings net income —/mo tax-free, plus the 100%-only education lever for your kids.
  • Floor is solid. Military pension + VA + Tricare means the family is covered before a single civilian paycheck.
  • Relocation is a money decision, not a vibe. A 50-state scan ranks TX > AL > VA > UT > FL — Huntsville-AL and Utah beat the original picks; the whole order pivots on whether your income is local or portable.
  • One hard deadline: the Ch.33 GI Bill transfer must be approved while on active duty — unrecoverable after 29 Feb 2028.

Confidence & what would change this

High on the financial floor and the federal benefit rules (cross-checked vs your own calculator + VA.gov). Verified on every relocation axis (property/income/homeschool law, BEA cost-of-living, Bankrate insurance, FBI crime); cleared-cyber salary bands are Tier-2.

State income tax on the civilian salary [Verified]

  • $0 on wages: TX, FL, TN (no income tax).
  • Taxes your civilian W-2: CO 4.4% · UT 4.55% · AL/GA/SC ~5% · VA 5.75% · MD ~7.5% (w/ local). On $150K that's ~$6.6K–$11K/yr — a permanent drag the no-tax states avoid (~—/yr at VA's rate).
  • Military-retirement pension is exempt in nearly all of these; the salary is what gets taxed.

Homeschool legal climate [Verified – HSLDA]

  • No-notice / lowest: TX (+ Prop 15 2025 constitutional amendment).
  • Low-reg: AL, UT, TN (church-umbrella route).
  • Moderate (notice + testing/eval): VA, FL, GA, SC, MD, CO.
  • Avoid (high-reg): MA, NY, PA, RI, VT.

36 months per eligible child, ages ~18–26. Paid to the student; no housing stipend. Monthly enrollment verification since Jan 2026. $0 at 70%.

Chapter 33 (Post-9/11) Transfer — TEB

  • 36-month shared pool — does include BAH-based housing + book stipend (unlike Ch.35).
  • Stack with Ch.35 per child (not same enrollment period); 48-mo cap if one person uses both.
  • Must be submitted AND approved while on active duty. Designate every dependent now (even $0) — you can re-allocate later but cannot add new dependents post-separation.
  • 10-yr/precluded retainability exemption likely applies — confirm with career counselor.

PPM / DITY move — 2026

Reimbursement is 100% of the Government Constructed Cost (GCC) for CY2026 (the temporary 130% bump expired 30 Sep 2025). [Verified – DFAS / DoD MAP 42-25(R)]

How profit actually works

  • Profit = GCC payment − your actual move costs. GCC scales with net weight hauled (capped at allowance) × distance.
  • E-8 w/ dependents allowance = 14,000 lb.
  • Reimbursement is taxable — DFAS withholds 22% federal (no FICA). Offset with documented expenses + certified weigh tickets.
