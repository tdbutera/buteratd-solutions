State income tax on the civilian salary [Verified]

  • $0 on wages: TX, FL, TN (no income tax).
  • Taxes your civilian W-2: CO 4.4% · UT 4.55% · AL/GA/SC ~5% · VA 5.75% · MD ~7.5% (w/ local). On $150K that's ~$6.6K–$11K/yr — a permanent drag the no-tax states avoid (~—/yr at VA's rate).
  • Military-retirement pension is exempt in nearly all of these; the salary is what gets taxed.

Chapter 33 (Post-9/11) Transfer — TEB

  • 36-month shared pool — does include BAH-based housing + book stipend (unlike Ch.35).
  • Stack with Ch.35 per child (not same enrollment period); 48-mo cap if one person uses both.
  • Must be submitted AND approved while on active duty. Designate every dependent now (even $0) — you can re-allocate later but cannot add new dependents post-separation.
  • 10-yr/precluded retainability exemption likely applies — confirm with career counselor.

PPM / DITY move — 2026

Reimbursement is 100% of the Government Constructed Cost (GCC) for CY2026 (the temporary 130% bump expired 30 Sep 2025). [Verified – DFAS / DoD MAP 42-25(R)]

How profit actually works

  • Profit = GCC payment − your actual move costs. GCC scales with net weight hauled (capped at allowance) × distance.
  • E-8 w/ dependents allowance = 14,000 lb.
  • Reimbursement is taxable — DFAS withholds 22% federal (no FICA). Offset with documented expenses + certified weigh tickets.
