Unified fabric — every site, one view

As Kings Cathedral standardizes beyond Maui, every site connects to one simple dashboard. It is a window for visibility and support — not a kill-switch. Each site keeps running on its own, the church owns all the hardware, and nothing stops working if a vendor ever leaves.
