# 08 · EDUCATION & GI BILL — Six-Kid Play

> The single largest financial swing in the plan, and it contains the **one hard, non-recoverable deadline.** Verified `99` §C, §G. Live: dashboard Education tab (countdowns).

## Two stackable programs

### Chapter 35 DEA (Survivors' & Dependents' Educational Assistance) — **100% P&T ONLY**
- **36 months per eligible child**, ages ~18–26 (no age cap if the P&T event is on/after Aug 1 2023).
- Rate **$1,574/mo** full-time (FY26, Oct 1 2025–Sep 30 2026). **Paid to the student. No housing stipend.**
- **6 kids × 36 mo × $1,574 = up to $339,984** — independent pools, not shared.
- Monthly enrollment verification required (since Jan 2026).
- **$0 at 70%.** This benefit is the core reason 100% P&T matters financially.

### Chapter 33 (Post-9/11 GI Bill) — Transfer of Education Benefits (TEB)
- **36-month shared pool** — *does* include BAH-based **housing allowance + book stipend** (the big advantage over Ch.35).
- Available regardless of disability rating (it's an earned benefit), so it's the floor even at 70%.
- Stack with Ch.35 per child, just **not for the same enrollment period**; 48-month combined cap if one person uses both.

## ⛔ The hard deadline (retire 29 Feb 2028)

**The Ch.33 TEB transfer must be SUBMITTED *and* APPROVED while still on active duty. It is impossible after retirement — unrecoverable.**

1. **Designate every dependent now** (spouse + all 6 kids) in milConnect, even a **$0 / 1-month** placeholder each. You can re-allocate months among *already-designated* dependents after separation, but you **cannot add a new dependent** later (only exception: a designated transferee dies).
2. **All dependents must be enrolled in DEERS** before the transfer can be made.
3. **Service obligation:** standard 4 additional years from the election; the **10-year/precluded-by-policy exemption** very likely applies (long service + high-year tenure) — **confirm with your career counselor** that you're retention-eligible and the OED won't outlast your retirement.
4. **Timing:** initiate ~**Feb–Mar 2027 (12 mo out)**; **approved by ~Aug–Sep 2027 (6 mo out)** with margin.

## Usage notes
- Spouse can use Ch.33 immediately; children's use generally after the member separates.
- Ch.35 (if P&T) does **not** require a transfer and isn't tied to the active-duty deadline — but designating Ch.33 to the kids now preserves maximum flexibility to mix both later.
